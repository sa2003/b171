Mr Md Ali Akhter Hossain, born on October 20, 1965, in Rajshahi, Bangladesh, is a distinguished civil engineer with over 34 years of experience. Graduating from Khulna University of Engineering and Technology in 1986, he later earned a postgraduate degree in Environmental Management from the University of Nottingham in 2002. Throughout his career at LGED, starting in 1989, Mr. Akhtar has held various key positions, contributing significantly to rural infrastructure development. Recognized for his dedication and competence, he has overseen numerous projects and received prestigious awards for his contributions. Married to Nilima Jahan Kakoli, he is a proud father of one son and one daughter. Mr. Ali Akhtar Hossain's commitment to Bangladesh's development is unwavering, as he continues to work towards the government's Vision 2041 through LGED, ensuring modernization and improved citizen services.

Ali Akhter Hossain: A Trailblazer in Engineering and Infrastructure Development

Ali Akhter Hossain's career within Bangladesh's Local Government Engineering Department (LGED) exemplifies dedication and impact. Starting as an Assistant Engineer, Hossain steadily rose through the ranks, overseeing critical projects like the Sustainable Rural Infrastructure Improvement Project (SRIIP) as Project Director. His leadership ensured the successful implementation of projects worth USD 135 million, benefiting rural communities and earning commendations from various stakeholders.

Hossain's strategic vision as Superintending Engineer paved the way for organizational growth and innovation within LGED. His current role as Additional Chief Engineer and Director of the Climate Resilient Local Infrastructure Centre (CReLIC) underscores his commitment to addressing climate change challenges in infrastructure development.

Throughout his career, Hossain has epitomized integrity and excellence, leaving an indelible mark on Bangladesh's engineering landscape. As he continues to lead LGED, his legacy of impactful leadership and sustainable development serves as inspiration for future generations in the field.
